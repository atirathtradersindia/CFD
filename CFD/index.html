The Vision Behind London Investment Markets

LIM has offices in the City of London and the Marshall Islands. It is the dedicated brokerage arm for the LIM Brokerage Group.

LIM was created by former UK Heads of Compliance and Qualified English lawyers to provide clients with the most attractive trading conditions whilst also offering the highest levels of legal and risk management.

We also have a passion to make the information and tuition being used by the major investment banks widely available to all. With that in mind, LIM has teamed up with leading market participants, Liquidity Providers and News Providers in order to provide you with all the knowledge/instruments you will need on your journey to becoming the best trader you can be.

We provide premier educational material, chatrooms, and webinars with industry experts to be by your side.

We at LIM want to provide best in the market education and support from beginner traders all the way through to professional and eligible counterparties.
