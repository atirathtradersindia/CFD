Quick Start Guide

Get up and running with your LIM Brokerage trading account in just 15 minutes. Follow this step-by-step guide to set up your account, explore the platform, and place your first trade.

Account Setup

Welcome to LIM Brokerage! Let's get your trading account set up so you can start trading Forex, Commodities, and CFDs.

1. Create Your Account

Visit our registration page and enter your email address, create a secure password, and verify your email.

Registration Form Illustration

2. Complete Your Profile

After verification, complete your profile with your personal details, trading experience, and financial information for compliance purposes.

3. Choose Your Account Type

Select the account type that fits your trading style: Micro Account for beginners, Standard Account for active traders, or ECN Account for professionals.

Pro Tip

Enable two-factor authentication during setup for enhanced security. This adds an extra layer of protection to your trading account and funds.

Platform Overview

Your trading platform is the command center for all your trading activities. Let's explore the key components.

Main Navigation

The top navigation provides quick access to all sections: Dashboard, Trading, Market Analysis, Account, and Support.

Trading Interface

The main trading area displays charts, order entry panels, and your open positions. Familiarize yourself with the layout.

Trading Platform Layout

Quick Actions

Use the toolbar for common tasks: placing new orders, adjusting positions, or accessing market analysis tools.

Pro Tip

Customize your trading layout by dragging and dropping widgets. Save different layouts for various trading strategies or market conditions.

Deposit Funds

Fund your trading account to start placing real trades. We offer multiple secure deposit methods.

1. Access Deposit Section

Navigate to the "Account" section and select "Deposit Funds" from the menu.

2. Choose Deposit Method

Select your preferred deposit method: Bank Transfer, Credit/Debit Card, or E-Wallet (Skrill, Neteller).

Deposit Methods Selection

3. Enter Deposit Amount

Enter the amount you wish to deposit, keeping in mind the minimum deposit requirements for your account type.

4. Confirm Deposit

Follow the prompts to complete your deposit. Most methods are processed instantly.

Pro Tip

Start with a smaller amount to familiarize yourself with the platform before committing larger sums. Consider using our demo account to practice first.

Market Analysis

Our advanced charting tools and market analysis features help you identify trading opportunities.

1. Access Market Watch

View real-time prices for all available instruments in the Market Watch window.

2. Use Charting Tools

Open charts for specific instruments and customize timeframes from 1 minute to monthly.

Interactive Chart Example

3. Apply Technical Indicators

Add moving averages, RSI, MACD, and other indicators from the technical analysis panel.

4. Access Market News

Stay informed with real-time economic news and analysis that may impact your trading decisions.

Pro Tip

Save your chart layouts with preferred indicators and timeframes. You can quickly apply these templates to other instruments.

Place Your First Trade

Now that you're familiar with the platform, let's place your first trade.

1. Select an Instrument

Choose from Forex pairs, Commodities, Indices, or Cryptocurrencies based on your analysis.

2. Set Order Parameters

Determine your trade size (lot size), set stop-loss and take-profit levels to manage risk.

Order Entry Panel

3. Choose Order Type

Select between Market Execution (instant) or Pending Orders (set conditions for entry).

4. Execute the Trade

Review your order details and click "Buy" or "Sell" to execute the trade.

Pro Tip

Always use stop-loss orders to limit potential losses. Never risk more than 1-2% of your account balance on a single trade.

Risk Management

Proper risk management is crucial for long-term trading success. Implement these strategies to protect your capital.

Position Sizing

Calculate appropriate position sizes based on your account balance and risk tolerance.

Stop-Loss Orders

Always use stop-loss orders to limit potential losses on every trade.

Risk Management Tools

Diversification

Spread your risk across different instruments and market sectors.

Risk-Reward Ratio

Aim for a minimum 1:2 risk-reward ratio on your trades to ensure profitability over time.

Pro Tip

Use our built-in risk calculator to determine appropriate position sizes based on your account balance and risk parameters.
